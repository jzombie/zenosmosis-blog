Side projects succeed when there is a low-friction loop between idea and validation.

Here are a few rituals I'm using this season: logging tight scopes, front-loading automation, and writing tiny postmortems for every session.

The examples use Hugo, Go, and a splash of TypeScript, but the rhythm works no matter what stack you prefer.
